Notes. The fridge and kitchen unit are bought products — no plywood/lumber for them. The headboard/pantry needs no frame lumber (it mounts on Panel C's deck). Legs are cut 1" short (16") — the leveling foot makes each an effective 17". Bottom rails sit at the leg bottoms (underside 1").

Joinery: plywood face joints get Ryobi R-series biscuits (R3 on the headboard shelves at 2"/7"/12", R1 on drawer/box corners and the nook divider); 2×2 frame corners get 2× 2" screws + a corner bracket. Full method in the plan's Joinery & Fastener Guide. The ½" sheet nests to ~95% — lay out carefully.
