Your RV bar level's scale is degrees — each mark = 1°, running −7 to +7. Just read the number the bubble sits at and enter it below; no conversion needed. The bubble always drifts toward the high side.

How to use it
  1. Park in the spot you actually want, engine off, parking brake on.
  2. Read both levels off the bed (they're screwed down — Section 6 of the build plan). Enter the degrees each one shows (each mark = 1°, scale −7 to +7), then set the toggle to the end/side that's low — the bubble drifts toward the high side, so the low side is the one it moves away from.
  3. Lay the block stacks just ahead of the low tires, drive up slowly, chock a wheel.
  4. Re-read the levels. Suspension squish and stack nesting mean one refinement round is normal.
