How to use it
  1. Park in the spot you actually want, engine off, parking brake on.
  2. Look at each RV bar level on the bed (they're screwed down — Section 6 of the build plan). Drag the matching bubble above to the same spot — it floats toward the HIGH side, so the low side is the opposite.
  3. Lay the block stacks just ahead of the low tires, drive up slowly, chock a wheel.
  4. Re-check the levels. Suspension squish and stack nesting mean one refinement round is normal.
